You have the right to withdraw from your subscription or purchase of Astro Premium within 14 days without giving any reason. The withdrawal period will expire after 14 days from the day of the conclusion of the contract.

To exercise your right to withdrawal, you must inform us of your decision to withdraw from this contract by an unequivocal statement by contacting the Discord user @giuliopime on https://discord.gg/astro-bot or by sending us an email at [hi@astro-bot.space](mailto:hi@astro-bot.space).
